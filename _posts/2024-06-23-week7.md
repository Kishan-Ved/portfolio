### <ins>Coding info</ins>

This week, I coded up the C++ backend for Splay Trees. The following methods were implemented:
- `SplayTree__zig()`
- `SplayTree__zig_zig()`
- `SplayTree__zig_zag()`
- `SplayTree__zag_zag()`
- `SplayTree__zag_zig()`
- `SplayTree_splay()`
- `SplayTree_insert()`
- `SplayTree_delete()`

### <ins>Relevant PRs:</ins>
